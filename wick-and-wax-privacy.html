Overview

Wick & Wax is a candle making calculator designed to help crafters determine wax and fragrance quantities. The app is developed by Papalote Labs. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

📱 Data You Provide

The app allows you to save candle recipes (container sizes, fragrance percentages, materials) and settings. This data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

📊 Advertising Data

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region)
  • App usage data

This data is collected by Google, not by us. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

How We Use Information

  • Local data: Your recipes and settings remain on your device to provide app functionality
  • Ad data: Used by Google to display relevant advertisements

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. The only external service that may receive data is Google AdMob for advertising purposes, as described above.

Data Storage

All app data (recipes, materials, settings) is stored locally on your device using standard secure storage. We do not operate servers that store your personal information.

Your Choices

  • Ad personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's privacy settings
  • Export data: You can export all your data (recipes, materials, settings) as a JSON file from the Settings screen
  • Delete data: Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data from your device

Children's Privacy

Wick & Wax is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us.

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any updates will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our practices, we're here to help.
